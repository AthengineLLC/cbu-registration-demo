Season Waivers

Your organization requires the following waivers for this season. Read each one, then check the agreement box. Both must be agreed to before you can continue.

Liability & Medical Release Waiver

Assumption of Risk. I understand that participation in baseball activities — including practices, training sessions, tournaments, and travel — involves inherent risks of injury. On behalf of myself and the player named in this registration, I knowingly and voluntarily assume all such risks.

Release. I release CBU Baseball, its coaches, directors, staff, and facility partners from any and all claims arising out of ordinary participation in team activities during the Summer 2026 season.

Medical Authorization. In the event of an injury or medical emergency where I cannot be reached, I authorize CBU staff to seek and consent to reasonable medical treatment for my player.

Insurance. I confirm the player is covered by medical insurance, and I understand CBU Baseball does not provide primary medical coverage for participants.

This is sample waiver language for demo purposes. Final waiver text is set by the organization in Registration Management.

Code of Conduct & Media Release

Conduct. Players and families agree to represent CBU Baseball with class — toward teammates, coaches, umpires, and opponents — at every event. Unsportsmanlike conduct by a player or family member may result in suspension from team activities.

Media Release. I grant CBU Baseball permission to use photos and video of the player taken at team events for team pages, recruiting materials, and social media.

Communication. I agree to receive team communications by email and text regarding schedules, events, and season logistics.

This is sample waiver language for demo purposes. Final waiver text is set by the organization in Registration Management.

Please agree to both waivers to continue.
